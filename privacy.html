Privacy Statement

Personal data we collect

The Exce-Lint add-in for Microsoft Excel does not collect personally identifiable information about you.

Other data collected

Exce-Lint currently collects no information of any kind. In order to track and improve Exce-Lint's effectiveness, Exce-Lint may in the future collect information to measure how features of Exce-Lint are being used and to evaluate their effectiveness, as well as details about the platform being used (e.g., Excel Online). No spreadsheet data will ever be collected or stored.
